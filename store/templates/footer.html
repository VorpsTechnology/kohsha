Kohsha

Koh•Sha derived from the Sanskrit word KOSHA which in Eastern philosophy identifies what encloses the pure consciousness of Spirit in its material manifestations. Hindu philosophy describes the five Koshas, as physical, energy, mental, wisdom, and bliss; each of which plays an important role as the five elements, Earth, Water, Fire, Air, and Eather. It is of utmost importance that one should be fed in the purest possible way as a tribute to keep your Kosha healthy and blissful. Our range of products is procured from hand-picked local farms from the most nurtured soil in India, the Himalayan Ranges. We ensure high nutrition value and purity in every product as we know you deserve nothing but the best.
